Personal E-mail Services

You live on the Internet and depend on your e-mail. You want an e-mail service that delivers. $Provider delivers—more than half a million e-mails a day, and we’re just getting started.

We know you’re serious about your messages. So are we. We’ve built an e-mail system just to handle your e-mail. Unlike the rest, your $Provider inbox is completely customized. Only want messages from people with valid SPF records? No problem. Don’t want to use realtime blackholes because your uncle’s e-mail always gets blocked? No problem. Have no idea what were talking about? No problem—just accept our default options. We give you the freedom that comes with running your own e-mail server—without the hassle or expense.

Choose the plan that works best for you. If you’re curious, just try one of our free plans. You can always upgrade later. If you need the features or the space, jump right into one of our value-priced plans.

All of our plans include access to our POP3, IMAP and webmail servers for downloading your incoming e-mail and access to our SMTP servers for sending your outgoing messages.
